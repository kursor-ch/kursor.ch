### 3.1 Funnel Ecosystem (Locked)

**Architecture:** Each funnel = specific pain point + specific target audience + visually stunning lead magnet + primary partner routing. One person can flow through multiple funnels = multiple partner commissions per lead. All funnels feed the same central database, enriching each lead profile over time. All funnels relay back to one central Kursor CH site.

**Strategic shifts (locked March 16, 2026):**

| Shift | Status | Detail |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Different audiences per funnel | LOCKED | Each funnel targets a different audience — NOT just "French people moving to Switzerland." Assurance targets current residents. Retraite targets people already working in CH. Only Work and Entrepreneur target pre-move candidates. |
| All funnels launch now | LOCKED | Don't wait to validate Work before launching others. Get all 5 rolling, then optimize through content, targeting, and tech iteration. |
| Kill personalized reports | LOCKED | **Fully automated, template-based reports.** No manual personalization per lead. Score + brief summary on screen instantly, generalized template report emailed automatically. This is the key to scaling from 5 leads/month to 500. |
| Unified funnel structure | LOCKED | All 5 funnels follow the same lightweight structure: catchy content → CTA → link → lead magnet (free interactive tool) → instant score + brief summary on screen → automated generalized report emailed. Then optimize per-funnel with audience-specific and niche-specific metrics. |

**Launch order & audiences:**

| # | Funnel | Primary partner | Target audience | Status |
|---|--------|----------------|----------------|--------|
| 1 | **WORK (Salarié)** | Recruteur | French-speaking professionals (28-42, CDI, 45-90k€) CONSIDERING moving to Switzerland. Pre-move. They know salaries are higher, don't know the hidden costs/traps. | LIVE |
| 2 | **LOGEMENT** | Immobilier / Régie | People who ARE moving or just arrived in Switzerland. Peri-move and recent arrivals. Actively looking for housing. Includes frontaliers looking across the border. | TO BUILD |
| 3 | **ASSURANCE (Complémentaires)** | Courtier assurances complémentaires | Anyone living or working in Switzerland who needs complémentaires (perte de gain, RC ménage, RC privée, juridique). Current residents, frontaliers, new arrivals. NOT LAMal — LAMal is government-regulated, not a private brokerage play. | TO BUILD |
| 4 | **RETRAITE (3ème pilier)** | 3ème pilier / Prévoyance | Anyone working in Switzerland who isn't optimizing their 3ème pilier. Could be there for years already. Frontaliers included. The pain: losing 3 500 CHF/year in tax deductions they didn't know about. | TO BUILD |
| 5 | **ENTREPRENEUR** | Fiduciaire | French-speaking professionals who want to create, transfer, or structure a business in Switzerland. Consultants, freelancers, e-commerce, SaaS founders. Pre-move AND already in CH wanting to go independent. | TO BUILD |

**Key insight:** Funnels 1 and 5 target pre-move candidates. Funnels 2, 3, 4 target people already in or actively entering Switzerland. This means the total addressable market is MUCH larger than "people thinking about moving" — it's the entire French-speaking population interacting with the Swiss system.

**Cross-funnel nurturing:** A Work lead gets nurtured into Logement → Assurance → Retraite over time. An Entrepreneur lead gets nurtured into Assurance → Retraite. One person = up to 4-5 partner commissions.

**Lead magnet principle:** Every lead magnet must be **visually stunning and interactive** — calculators, simulators, interactive diagnostic tools that give immediate value. Not forms. Not PDFs. This is how we capture more data and more qualified leads than competitors. Each lead magnet to be designed in the Brain project.

**Central site:** All funnels relay back to kursor.ch as the central hub. Site needs to be built (currently no central site exists — only Tally form and TikTok). This is a Hands project priority.

**Each funnel needs (to be designed in Brain project):**
- Target audience deep-dive (persona, pain points, where they are in their journey)
- Lead magnet design (interactive tool, not a form)
- Question set and data captured
- Scoring logic (if applicable)
- Partner routing rules
- Content strategy (hooks specific to this funnel's audience and pain point)
- Template report structure
- Niche-specific optimization metrics
